EXTERNAL LINKS — RTTY • TELETYPE • HISTORY • ORGANIZATIONS
This page is a work in progress. The original RTTY.COM links page contained many references accumulated over decades; some are no longer active. Links here have been verified as working. If you know of a link that should be included, contact W2TTY.
Community
GreenKeys Email List mailman.qth.net/mailman/listinfo/greenkeys
The primary active community for teletype collectors, restorers, and RTTY enthusiasts. Covers all aspects of mechanical teleprinters, terminal units, restoration, history, and buying and selling. Hosted at QTH.net.
ARRL RTTY Resources arrl.org — RTTY PDF
The American Radio Relay League’s RTTY reference document, covering operating practices, contest information, and technical background.
BARTG — British Amateur Radio Teledata Group bartg.org.uk
The UK organization dedicated to RTTY and digital modes. Active contest program and technical resources for European operators.
Software
MMTTY hamsoft.ca/pages/mmtty.php
The most widely used Windows RTTY software decoder. Essential for decoding ITTY streams on Windows with a virtual audio cable.
fldigi sourceforge.net/projects/fldigi
Cross-platform multi-mode digital decoder (Windows, Mac, Linux). Includes RTTY mode with configurable shift and baud rate.
VB-Cable (Windows virtual audio) vb-audio.com/Cable
Free virtual audio cable for Windows. Routes audio from a media player to a decoder without a physical connection.
BlackHole (Mac virtual audio) existential.audio/blackhole
Virtual audio routing for macOS. The Mac equivalent of VB-Cable.
History & Reference
Antique Radio Forums antiqueradio.org
Forums covering vintage radio and communications equipment, including occasional discussion of teletype machines and associated gear.
